Transforming Sales Performance:
From Zero to 400% Lead Conversion Growth

A case study in building revenue engines for B2B startups

Company cQuant.io
Industry Renewable Energy Analytics
Role Founding Sales Development Representative
Timeline January 2023 - March 2024

Our SDR transformed how we approach new business. We're now in active discussions with major utilities, and our brand recognition at industry events has completely changed. People know who we are before we even introduce ourselves.

— VP of Business Development, cQuant.io

The Challenge

When I joined cQuant.io as their first SDR, the company faced a critical growth inflection point. This seed-stage energy analytics startup had innovative technology but struggled to translate it into pipeline momentum.

The reality check:

Zero market presence

Target utilities had never heard of us

Broken sales motion

Account Executives spent 70% of their time on cold prospecting instead of closing deals

No infrastructure

Zero outreach tools, tracking systems, or established processes

Stagnant pipeline

Minimal new business generation despite strong product-market fit

The Mission

Build a scalable revenue engine that would:

  • Generate consistent, qualified pipeline growth
  • Establish cQuant.io as a recognized player in the utility sector
  • Free up AEs to focus on deal progression and closure
  • Create repeatable processes for future team scaling

The Approach

1. Engineered Hyper-Personalized Outreach

  • Designed multi-touch campaigns specifically addressing utility pain points around grid optimization and renewable integration
  • Created industry-specific messaging that resonated with technical buyers and C-suite executives
  • Developed a LinkedIn engagement strategy that turned cold connections into warm conversations

2. Built a Modern Sales Tech Stack

  • Implemented Apollo.io for intelligent prospecting and automation
  • Designed automated nurture sequences that maintained personalization at scale
  • Created dashboards for real-time performance tracking and optimization

3. Revolutionized the Sales Process

  • Introduced a rigorous lead qualification framework (BANT+) that increased AE efficiency by 3x
  • Established clear handoff protocols between SDR and AE functions
  • Built a feedback loop for continuous messaging refinement based on prospect responses

4. Executed Strategic Market Penetration

  • Mapped industry events to outreach campaigns, ensuring prospects knew us before meeting us
  • Launched a utility-focused campaign that opened doors to previously unreachable accounts
  • Created "surround sound" effect through coordinated email, LinkedIn, and event touchpoints

The Results

Quantitative Wins

400% Lead Conversion Increase
5 Major Utilities in Pipeline
60% AE Time Saved
Q3 Revenue Target Exceeded

Qualitative Transformation

  • Market Recognition: Prospects at conferences began approaching us instead of the reverse
  • Sales Efficiency: AEs reported their highest close rates ever due to better-qualified opportunities
  • Scalable Foundation: Created playbooks and processes that enabled successful hiring of new SDR and current AEs

Key Learnings

This experience reinforced that successful B2B sales development isn't about volume—it's about precision. By combining deep market understanding with modern sales technology and relentless optimization, even a seed-stage startup can compete effectively against established players.

The transformation at cQuant.io proves that with the right strategy and execution, a single SDR can fundamentally change a company's growth trajectory.
